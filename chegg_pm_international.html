Chegg International Expansion Strategy

Here’s an overview of how we scaled Chegg Study into international markets while balancing growth, operational complexity, and localized user needs:

Key Highlights:

  • Prioritized market entry based on TAM, competitive dynamics, and LTV projections
  • Rolled out local currency presentment (LCP) and optimized regional pricing
  • Streamlined product localization with scalable tech architecture
  • Balanced regional customization with core platform consistency to maximize margin

Results:

⚡ Achieved double-digit growth in new international subscriber segments within the first year of rollout.

Deep Dive:

At Chegg, the business provided an OKR centered around international conversion rate growth. Our goal was to demonstrate conversion growth in international markets while competing against ChatGPT.

Through a program of continuous discovery that I had instituted with my designer and eng lead, we already had a pretty good idea of the international opportunity space. Customer journey mapping showed we had significant drop-off on our pricing and packaging page for international users. Improving passthrough rate on this page would drive CVR overall.
